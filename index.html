Spatial profile
Two parafoveal trials: 3° and 7° eccentricity at 460 nm.

3° trial: fixate the + cross on screen. The disk appears above it.

7° trial: hold one finger beside the phone at arm's length, roughly one screen-width to the right. Keep your gaze on your fingertip — the disk will flicker on the screen in your peripheral vision. Do not look at it.

Tap when the peripheral flicker feels calmest. A red flash means the reading was rejected — just wait and tap again.
Fixation on your finger is critical for the 7° trial. Any drift toward the disk inflates the reading and will be rejected automatically.
